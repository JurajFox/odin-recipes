Bolognese Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Pasta Spaghetti
  • Minced Meat
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Wine
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Add oil to pan and put on medium heat.
  2. Add chopped vegetables.
  3. Add minced meat.
  4. When meat is brown add wine. Let the wine reduce and add tomato sauce and water.
  5. Put on simmer for about 2 hours with occasional stirring.
  6. When the water is almost gone add milk.
  7. Simmer about 10 more minutes and serve with spaghetti
