Lasagna Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Pasta Lasagna
  • Minced Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Besamel Sauce

Steps

  1. Add oil to pan and put on medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions.
  3. Add minced meat.
  4. When meat is brown add tomato sauce and water. Let the sauce reduce and put away.
  5. In a tray put layers of ingredients so they cover each other: pasta, besamel sauce, meat sauce, cheese
  6. On the last layer of pasta put only besamel sauce and cheese
  7. Preheat Oven to 200 Celsius
  8. Bake covered with aluminum foil for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for another 20 minutes
