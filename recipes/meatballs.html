Meatballs Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Minced Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Rice
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Besamel Sauce

Steps

  1. Combine chopped garlic, minced meat and rice into balls.
  2. Add oil to pan and put on medium heat.
  3. Fry the meatballs so they are brown from all sides.
  4. Take out meat and to the same pan add more oil and chopped onion
  5. When onion is soft add tomato sauce and meatballs. Add water to cover the meatballs.
  6. Cook until sauce is reduced and meatballs are cooked. Optionally take out meatballs and blend the sauce with stick blender.
  7. Serve with mashed potatoes.
